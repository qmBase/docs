# Target Audience

## Primary audience
- Quality Managers
- Quality Officers
- Process Owners
- Internal Auditors

Characteristics:
- Domain knowledge in quality management
- Limited technical background
- Time-constrained
- Risk-averse

## Secondary audience
- IT administrators
- Consultants supporting QM implementations
- External auditors (read-only)

## Non-audience
- Software developers
- Sales prospects
- End customers of the user's organization

Documentation must assume:
- Familiarity with quality management concepts
- No familiarity with this specific software
