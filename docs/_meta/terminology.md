# Terminology & Definitions

The following terms are used consistently throughout the documentation.

## Core terms

**Document**
A controlled digital record managed within the system.

**Process**
A defined sequence of activities with an owner and purpose.

**Qualification**
A documented capability or competence assigned to a person.

**Training**
An activity that may result in one or more qualifications.

**Validity Period**
The time range during which a qualification is considered valid.

**Evidence**
Any document or record used to demonstrate completion or compliance.

## Language rules
- “User” refers to a person using the software
- “Organization” refers to the customer company
- “System” refers to the SaaS application
- Avoid synonyms for defined terms

- Canonical terminology is defined in **German**
- German terms take precedence over English
- Do not translate locked terms inconsistently

Example:
- Always use **„Qualifikation“**
- Do NOT alternate with:
  - „Kompetenz“
  - „Befähigung“
